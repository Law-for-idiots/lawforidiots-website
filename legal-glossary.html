Legal Glossary

Welcome to the Legal Glossary, here you can find all the legal terminology that is presented to you in human-friendly manner.

D
Department of Labour

The Department of Labour is a government department in South Africa that is responsible for protecting the rights of workers and employers. They do this by enforcing laws, providing information and services, and resolving disputes.

L
Labour Inspector

A Labour Inspector is a person who makes sure that employers follow the laws that protect workers. They do this by inspecting workplaces, talking to workers and employers, and investigating complaints.

Labour Relations Act

The Labour Relations Act is a law that protects the rights of workers in South Africa. It says that workers have the right to fair pay, safe working conditions, and freedom from discrimination. It also says that workers have the right to join a trade union and to bargain collectively with their employer.
